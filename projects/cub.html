Competition for the Uses of Biomass [CUB]

The CUB [Competition for the Uses of Biomass] model is a system dynamics simulation of the competing end-uses of biomass (for biopower, biofuel, traditional uses, and advanced products) and the effect of that competition on biomass and bioenergy markets.

Selected Publications
