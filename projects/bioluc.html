Bioenergy Land Use Change [BioLUC]

The BioLUC [Bioenergy Land Use Change] model is a system dynamics simulation of the drivers and impacts of biomass production on global land use change. It aims to integrate land-use-change, life-cycle- analysis, and supply-chain models in order to assess global sustainability impacts of domestic bioenergy production.
