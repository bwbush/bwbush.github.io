Projects

Here is a list of projects:
  • Logical Reasoning on Semistructured Databases: logical representation and querying of a semi-structured database
  • Risk Analysis of Renewable Energy Investments: analysis of variance-related effects of renewable energy
  • Competition for the Uses of Biomass [CUB]: a system dynamics simulation of the competition for biomass resources
  • Automated Analysis of Renewable Energy Datasets: general purpose algorithms for automated analysis of datasets
  • Bioenergy Land Use Change [BioLUC]: a system dynamics model of the drivers and impacts of biomass production on global land use change.
  • Spatiotemporal Analysis of Renewable Energy Datasets: a geostatistical approach to increasing spatial and temporal resolution of renewable energy resource data
  • Scenario Evaluation, Regionalization, & Analysis [SERA]: a geospatially and temporally oriented renewable fuels infrastructure analysis model
  • Biomass Scenario Model [BSM]: a biomass-to-bioenergy supply-chain system dynamics simulation
  • Weather Impacts: analysis of weather impacts on critical infrastucture
  • HYDRA Population & Economic Modeling: a service-oriented architecture for agent-based infrastructure modeling
  • Critical Infrastructure Protection Decision Support System [CIPDSS]: a simulation-based decision support system for assessing infrastructure protection risks
  • à la carte: a simulation-based analysis tool for evaluating massively parallel computing platforms
  • TRansportation ANalysis SIMulation System [TRANSIMS]: a comprehensive microsimulation of vehicular traffic in urban areas
  • Empirical Characterization of Infrastructure Networks [INCCA]: a graph-theoretic analysis of infrastructure neworks
  • Interdependent Energy Infrastructure Simulation System [IEISS]: an agent-based energy-system simulation tool
  • Infrastructure Modeling, Simulation, and Analysis: modeling, simulation, and analysis of critical infrastructure networks
  • Nuclear Structure & Reactions: research on nuclear structure and nuclear reactions
